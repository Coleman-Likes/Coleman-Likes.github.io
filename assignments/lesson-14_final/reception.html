Reception Hall

Wedding Receptions
We have a beautiful reception room that is ideal for weddings. The reception and sitting areas are decorated with a temple history theme and we can add your wedding colors to the decor . Our wedding planners can help with all of the details to make your day stress free, and can help you order flowers and decorations. Our staff will set up your wedding and can provide full banquets from our food services department. (See sample menu below)
