St. George, Utah Temple.

The St. George Utah Temple is a temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. It was the first temple built in Utah when the pioneers migrated west. It was completed in 1877 and is the oldest active temple in use today. The temple currently has three ordinance rooms and 18 sealing rooms.
